Starfish & Duck

Starfish and Duck are Parent and Tot levels. Parents will be attending the class along with their babies and toddlers. Parents wil be educated on water safety and how to handle and assist their babies in and around the water. Babies and toddlers will have their first experiences around the water.

  • Class Size: Up to 10 parents and toddlers.
  • Age group: 6 months to 2 and a half years.

After Preschool

Students will move into the Swim Kids Program after they turn 6 years old.

6 and Older

After your child turns 6 years old, they will be moving into the Swim Kids Program. Here's a guide on leel equivalencies of preschool to Swim Kids.

  • Starfish to Sea Otter -> Swim Kids 1
  • Salamander / Sunfish -> Swim Kids 2
  • Crocodile / Whale -> Swim Kids 3
  • Preschool 4 / 5 -> Swim Kids 4
Swim Kids
Swim Clubs

If you child has completed Whale of higher, they have the option to continue their learning through swim clubs. Swimming club programs focus on power and speed. The strokes learned will be a more competitive style compared to the swim kids style.

    Pros
  • More Challenging, Better Fitness, Better technique
  • Swim clubs are team oriented, develop friendships
  • More structured and strict, builds character
    Cons
  • No water safety
  • more expensive and time consuming
