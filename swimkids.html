Swim Kids is designed for children 6 years old and upwards. A total of 10 levels are in the curriculum and each level consists of three categories: Fitness, Water Safety and Swimming.

As students progress through the level they will be able to swim increased distances up to half a kilometer. Every level will introduce new water safety items. Upon completion, each student will know three resting strokes and two power strokes.

Swim Kids 1 - 2

In this level, students will learn be introduced to floats, glides and rhythmic breathing. This level is meant for any student who has little or no swimming experience; the perfect place to start for beginners. If your child has previous swimming experience, you may visit your local swimming pool for a free swimming accessment. Student who complete level one will be able to swim up to five meters and perform front and back floats.

  • Class Size: 3 - 6 Swimmers
Fitness

Students will be introduced to flutter kick. Kicking is the primary source of power for swimming and it is crucial for students to build a strong flutter kick. Flutter kick can be described as a motion of alternating legs in up and down motion starting from the hip. You may find exercises and drills to improve flutter kick in the link below

Flutter Kick Drills
Water Safety

Students will be introduced to a pool environment for the first time. Many instructors take their students on a tour around the pool area to point out dangers and meeting areas.

Students will need to identify when it is safe to enter the pool and choose the correct entry. Factors including depth of water, amount of swimmers and type of pool. Students will learn to choose from wade entry, ladder entry, slip-in and jump-ins.

Finally students will learn to blow bubbles through their mouth and nose and the ability to submerge their head completely underwater.

Swimming Skills

Students will learn the floating, gliding and gliding with kick. Floats are performed for a minimum of 3 seconds and glides are performed for a minimum of 5 meters. Student may have minimal help from instructors to start their glides. Finally students must complete a front swim of 5 meters. Guides on floating and gliding can be found below.

Floats and Glides

Swim Kids 3 - 4

In level 2, students will further build on skills they've learned in level one. The required distance swim has been increased to 10 meter. Introduction to deep water activities and lifejackets are also part of this level. It is recommended students do not use goggles until level 5.

  • Class Size: 3 - 6 Swimmers
